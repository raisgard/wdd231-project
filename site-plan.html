Site Plan: Healthy Living with Cassava Bread

Site Name

Healthy Living with Cassava Bread

This name reflects the website’s focus on promoting health and wellness through cassava bread. It is simple, descriptive, and connects both nutrition and lifestyle in one phrase.

Optional domain: healthylivingcassava.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to educate and inspire visitors to live healthier lives through cassava bread. It will provide nutritional information, recipes, lifestyle tips, and resources for individuals and families seeking healthy food alternatives.

Scenarios

  • What are the nutritional benefits of eating cassava bread compared to regular wheat bread?
  • How can I prepare quick and healthy recipes using cassava bread?
  • Where can I find wellness tips that incorporate cassava bread into a balanced lifestyle?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Forest Green (#3b6e4d) – Used for headings, buttons, and accents.

Secondary Color: Golden Beige (#e2b659) – Used for highlights, borders, and accent backgrounds.

Background Color: Light Cream (#fdfdf9) – Used as the page background for a clean, natural feel.

Typography

Heading Font: Playfair Display – Used for all headings to give a modern, organic, and elegant feel.

Body Font: Roboto – Used for body text for readability and clarity.
